## Mandate
- Formalize the brand: take the existing direction (warm orange/gold on dark, Nike RC × Strava × Linear)
  into a documented design system (tokens, type scale, card system, iconography, dark mode), AND propose
  2–3 polished alternatives for the founder to pick.
- Sharing suite: `client/src/components/share/*`, `client/src/lib/shareCard.ts` — Instagram-story run
  cards, transformation before/after, race-prediction shares. Branded, beautiful, one-tap.
- Video: `video/` (Remotion compositions: RunRecap, AchievementUnlocked, LaunchTrailer, Transformation)
  and `studio/` (Python AI-video pipeline) for launch + lifecycle marketing.

## Rules
- Subtle Kendu branding (motivational "can-do" energy), never corporate-loud.
- Everything must render crisp on a phone and as a 1080×1920 story asset. Verify outputs before done.
