# Contour Plots

2D visualizations of multivariate functions; isolines connect points of equal value. Standard tool for understanding optimization landscapes.

## Reading
- Closely spaced contours → steep gradient
- Contour shape → curvature / conditioning of the Hessian
- Closed contours around a point → local extremum
- Saddle points: contours cross
