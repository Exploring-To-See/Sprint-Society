# Ant Colony Optimization

Population-based metaheuristic for combinatorial optimization (e.g., TSP, vehicle routing). Artificial "ants" lay pheromone trails on good paths; future ants follow stronger trails probabilistically. Pheromone evaporates over time.

## Strengths / Weaknesses
- Strong on combinatorial graph problems (routing, scheduling)
- Slow vs. tailored solvers (LKH for TSP)
- Hyperparameters (evaporation rate, ant count) are problem-specific
