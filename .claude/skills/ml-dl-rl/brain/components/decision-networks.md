# Decision Networks (Influence Diagrams)

Bayesian networks extended with **decision** nodes (rectangles) and **utility** nodes (diamonds). The optimal decision policy maximizes expected utility.

Solved by variable elimination over chance nodes plus argmax over decision nodes. Naturally encodes [[../concepts/value-of-information]].
