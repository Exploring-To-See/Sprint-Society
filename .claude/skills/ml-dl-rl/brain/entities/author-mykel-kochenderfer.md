# Mykel J. Kochenderfer

Stanford professor, lead author of the Stanford SISL "algorithms" textbook series.

## Profile
- **Affiliation:** Stanford University, Aeronautics & Astronautics; Stanford Intelligent Systems Lab (SISL)
- **PhD:** University of Edinburgh
- **Earlier:** MIT Lincoln Laboratory
- **Research focus:** Decision making under uncertainty, validation of safety-critical autonomy, aviation, autonomous driving

## Authored Works in Brain
- [[book-dmu]] — Decision Making Under Uncertainty (2015, lead author)
- [[book-opt]] — Algorithms for Optimization (2019, with Wheeler)
- [[book-dm]] — Algorithms for Decision Making (2022, with Wheeler & Wray)
- [[book-val]] — Algorithms for Validation (2026, with Katz, Corso, Moss)
