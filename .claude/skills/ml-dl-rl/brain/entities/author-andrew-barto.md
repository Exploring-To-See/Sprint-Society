# Andrew G. Barto

Co-founder of modern reinforcement learning; Turing Award 2024 with Richard Sutton.

## Profile
- **Affiliation:** University of Massachusetts Amherst (Professor Emeritus)
- **Notable contributions:** Foundational RL framework with Sutton, actor-critic methods, RL connections to neuroscience and psychology (dopamine ↔ TD-error)
- **Award:** ACM Turing Award 2024 (with [[author-richard-sutton]])

## Authored Works in Brain
- [[book-rl-suttonbarto]] — Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction (2nd ed.)

Endorsed [[book-marl]] (multi-agent RL) calling it "the perfect starting point for a grounding in the field."
