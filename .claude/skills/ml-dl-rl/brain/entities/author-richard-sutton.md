# Richard S. Sutton

Co-founder of modern reinforcement learning; Turing Award 2024 with Andrew Barto.

## Profile
- **Affiliation:** University of Alberta; DeepMind / Keen Technologies
- **Notable contributions:** TD learning, Q-learning lineage, policy gradient (REINFORCE was Williams; Sutton developed actor-critic), Dyna integrated planning + learning, options framework
- **Award:** ACM Turing Award 2024 (with [[author-andrew-barto]]) for foundational work on RL
