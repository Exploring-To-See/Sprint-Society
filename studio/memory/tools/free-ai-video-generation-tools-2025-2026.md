# Free AI Video Generation Tools 2025-2026

**Category:** tools
**Saved:** 2026-05-14
**Tags:** ai, video-generation, free, tools

---


**Best free-tier options for generating video clips:**
- **HailuoAI/MiniMax** — Best free tier, strong motion realism, daily free generations
- **Kling AI** — Strong motion, good for dynamic scenes, free credits on signup
- **Pika 2.0** — Quick iterations, special effects (melting, crushing), daily free credits
- **Luma Dream Machine** — ~30 free gens/month, good camera movement
- **Wan 2.2** — Open-source, cheapest at scale via Replicate

**For music:** Suno (free daily credits), Udio (free daily credits)
**For voice/audio:** ElevenLabs (10k chars/month free), Whisper (fully free, open-source)
