# Best Performing AI Video Styles on Social Media

**Category:** techniques
**Saved:** 2026-05-14
**Tags:** trends, social-media, reels, content

---


**What stops the scroll:**
- Hyper-surreal transformations (objects morphing)
- Impossible camera movements (continuous shots that cant exist in reality)
- Music-synced visuals (cuts precisely on beat drops)
- One clear impossible moment per clip
- Short: 5-15s for loops, 30-60s max for narrative
- Posted native, not labeled as AI art

**For run clubs specifically:**
- Slow-mo beauty shots of runners
- Fast flash cuts with stats overlaid
- Before/after transformation content
- Cold plunge / recovery content (trending)
- Testimonial clips with branded lower-thirds
