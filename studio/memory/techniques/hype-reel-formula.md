# Hype Reel Formula

**Category:** techniques
**Saved:** 2026-05-14
**Tags:** editing, hype, formula, reels

---


**The proven formula for teaser/hype reels:**
1. Open with 1-2s of black or dark with ambient sound/bass hit
2. Flash cuts (0.3-0.8s each) of the best action moments
3. Each cut synced to a beat or bass hit
4. Text slam mid-video (brand name, big and bold)
5. Brief pause/breath after text (0.5s)
6. Final CTA text (Coming Soon / Join Now / Link in Bio)
7. End with logo + footer (1-2s)

**Key rules:**
- Never more than 6 consecutive cuts without a breath moment
- Orange/red tint on action footage = aggression/energy
- White flash between cuts = higher energy
- Slow-mo mid-sequence = dramatic contrast
- Total duration: 8-15s for max rewatchability
