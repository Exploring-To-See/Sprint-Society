# Sprint Society — Beta Tester Guide

## What is Sprint Society?

Sprint Society is an AI-powered run club app. Think of it as your personal running coach + community — all in one place.

You join, it learns how you run, and it builds everything around YOUR pace, YOUR goals, YOUR level.

---

## Core Features

**1. Smart Runner Profile**
- No long forms. Tap-based onboarding — takes 30 seconds
- Connects to Strava to auto-pull your runs
- Instantly classifies your tier (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)

**2. AI Coach**
- Chat with your personal running coach anytime
- It knows your VDOT, pace zones, training load, injury risk
- Gives personalized advice — not generic internet tips

**3. Personalized Pace Zones**
- Your exact easy/tempo/interval/race paces calculated from YOUR data
- Updates as you improve
- Based on real running science (Jack Daniels method)

**4. Weekly Challenges**
- Fresh challenges every week (bodyweight, nutrition, hydration, technique)
- Earn XP, level up, build streaks
- Compete with the community

**5. Transformation Journey**
- Week-by-week plan to get faster
- Adapts to your progress
- Visual progress tracking

**6. Shareable Run Cards**
- Beautiful Instagram-story-sized cards after your runs
- One tap to share your achievements
- Makes you look good 😤

## Why Use This Over Just Strava?

| Strava | Sprint Society |
|--------|---------------|
| Shows what you did | Tells you what to do next |
| Generic community | Coached community |
| No training plan | Personalized plan |
| Just data | Data + action |

## How to Get Started

1. Open the app link
2. Sign up (takes 10 seconds)
3. Connect Strava (optional but recommended)
4. Complete your runner profile (tap-based, 30 seconds)
5. Start chatting with your AI coach or check your pace zones
