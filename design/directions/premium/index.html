AI-Coaching-Premium — 5 Home Variations

Same premium language (cinematic dark, glass + lightness-ladder depth, coach voice, violet×orange discipline), five different organizing ideas. Informed by 10 recent AI-UI/UX skill repos. Pick the strongest execution — it gets rolled across every core page.

The Coach Speaks
The screen is the coach. First-person voice is the sole hero; data summoned via tappable suggestion chips. Most differentiated, most on-brief.
